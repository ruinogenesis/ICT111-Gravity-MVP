RSU students: sell what you can't take. Find what you need.

The problem: students moving out lose time and money selling used furniture, appliances, and textbooks in scattered, unsearchable Facebook and LINE groups — posts get buried and working items get thrown away.

Who it's for: verified Rangsit University students only — sellers moving out, buyers moving in. We keep it RSU-only so both sides always know they are dealing with a real, verified student.

Safer meetups, not promises: Gravity verifies students and keeps your contact private until both sides agree — but always meet in busy campus spots (library front, dorm lobbies) and bring a friend for big items. Gravity connects you; the meetup is yours.

Verification = RSU email + one-time in-person student ID check (no ID stored). Verification is simulated in this demo. No personal contact details are shown on this page — that is by design.

What happens next?
  1. The seller sees your request in their Gravity account.
  2. If they accept, both of you see each other's RSU email — nothing else, and nobody else sees it.
  3. You email each other to agree a time at the pickup area. Meet in busy campus spots.

Register — RSU students only

Why RSU-only? Every account is a real Rangsit University student, verified by RSU email plus a one-time in-person student ID check. We look at your ID once — we never store ID photos or numbers. This is what makes meeting a stranger from Gravity different from meeting a stranger from a Facebook group.

We collect only your display name and RSU email. Your email is shown to exactly one person — a buyer/seller you both agreed to connect with — and never appears on public pages.
