## Testing Notes
Testers follow tasks T1–T6 from /docs/experiment-script.md:
1. Read the homepage and say what Gravity is for.
2. Post a listing (desk, 800 THB, Good, Dorm A) — expect a success message and the desk in the list.
3. Find a textbook under 300 THB using search or the category filter.
4. Open its detail — note the RSU verified badge.
5. Explain Available / Reserved / Sold.
6. Press "Request contact" and explain what happens next.

Records save in the tester's browser only. Facilitator: press "Reset demo data" between testers.
