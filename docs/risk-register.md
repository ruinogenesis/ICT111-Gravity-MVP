## Overall Risk Decision
The prototype is **safe to continue building with mitigation**. No high-likelihood/high-severity risk exists: the two high-severity risks (R-01 contact leak, R-03 ID data drift) are held at low likelihood by locked design decisions, and every open risk has a concrete mitigation already scheduled — five land in the Lab 10 Sprint 1 backlog (R-02 form constraints, R-04 XSS fix, R-05 admin separation, R-07 clarity fixes, R-09 duplicate warning), and two are wording/process rules applied immediately (R-06, R-08). No fixed requirement FR-01–16 changes; one clarification to FR-15's contact channel is recorded in `/docs/updated-requirements-note.md`. Risks will be rechecked after Sprint 2 (Lab 11) and before final release (Lab 14).
